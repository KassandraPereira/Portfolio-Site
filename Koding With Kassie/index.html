about me

From Sales Rep to Web Developer a little bit about me...

My journey to becoming a Web Developer is a little less conventional than most. For the past 12 years I've worked in Sales in the Fashion and Beauty industries, but never felt truly connected to this career path. For years I built websites and blogs using platforms, but never thought about pursuing a career in development and coding until last Summer. As a creative person I love to see my work come to life on my screen and as a forever student I love that as a programmer and developer you learn something new everyday. I have finally found my passion and together we can build something amazing!

When I'm not creating I'm spending time with my husband and two little boys in sunny Miami.
